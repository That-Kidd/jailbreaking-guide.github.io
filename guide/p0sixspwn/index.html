P0sixspwn Jailbreak Method

This method only supports All Devices on iOS 6.1.3 - iOS 6.1.6.
This Jailbreak Method may not work on some device models.
Before continuing this guide, please make sure you have backed up your iOS Device via iTunes and iCloud.
You must have macOS or Windows installed to jailbreak with P0sixspwn. Using Wine will result in an error.
Also feel free to check out the Jailbreaking Essentials
For this guide you will need to following;
  • Computer running macOS or Windows (VM might work)
  • P0sixspwn
  • iOS Device running iOS 6.1.3 - 6.1.6
Step 1: Downloading P0sixspwn
  1. Download P0sixspwn
  2. On macOS, right-click on p0sixspwn.app and click 'Open'. On Windows, right-click on p0sixspwn.exe and click 'Run as Administrator'
Step 2: Jailbreak
  1. Connect your iOS Device with your Charge/Sync cable
  2. Click Jailbreak
  3. Put your device in DFU mode by following the onscreen prompts
If the Jailbreak fails, try using a different Charge/Sync cable or redownloading P0sixspwn. You can also consult the Troubleshooting section of the guide.
Step 3: Cydia
  1. Now that you are jailbroken, open Cydia
  2. Once it's done preparing the filesystem, reboot your device
  3. Open Cydia once more and complete all upgrades
Once you've completed all upgrades, you are now free to use your Jailbreak as you please.

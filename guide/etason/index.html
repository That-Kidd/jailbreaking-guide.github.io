Etason Jailbreak Method

This method only supports All Devices on iOS 8.4.1. If you are on an older version of iOS 8 use TaiG instead.
Before continuing this guide, please make sure you have backed up your iOS Device via iTunes and iCloud.
For this guide you will need to following;
  • The EtasonJB IPA
  • Cydia Impactor
  • A Free Developer Apple ID
Step 1: Impactor
  1. Connect your iOS device to your Mac or PC (Apple OEM cables are recommended)
  2. Start Cydia Impactor, then drag the EtasonJB IPA onto the window
  3. You will be prompted for your Apple ID credentials (this one has to be enrolled in a beta program (free or paid)
  4. Once you've entered your credentials in correctly, Cydia Impactor should install Yalu onto your iOS Device.
If this fails, try again with your correct Apple ID or a different charge/sync cable, if you experience any other issues refer to the Troubleshooting section.
Step 2: Etason Jailbreak
  1. Trust the certificate in Settings -> General -> Profiles & Device Management
  2. Open EtasonJB on your iOS Device and press begin.
    3. If you Cydia does not appear, try again.
  1. After your device resprings, you should have Cydia installed on your Home Screen
  2. Launch Cydia and let it (re)load its packages. If you are prompted for an upgrade, tap Upgrade All

Once all upgrades are completed and you've resprung, you are free to use any (compatible) Cydia tweaks

You can now uninstall EtasonJB since you dont need it anymore.

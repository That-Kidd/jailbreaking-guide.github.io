TaiG Jailbreak Method

This method only supports All Devices on iOS 8.0 - iOS 8.4. If you are on iOS 8.4.1 use Etason, update to iOS 9.3.5 and use Phoenix, or update to iOS 10.3.3 and use Double H3lix or H3lix instead.
Before continuing this guide, please make sure you have backed up your iOS Device via iTunes and iCloud.

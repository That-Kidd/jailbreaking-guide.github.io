Blob saving


Step 1: Getting the ECID
  1. Connect your iOS device to your Mac or PC (Apple OEM cables are recommended)
  2. Start Itunes, then click on the little iPhone icon
  3. You will a summary of your iPhone, click the serial number 2 to reveal the ECID
  4. Save the ECID somewhere for later

Step 2: Getting the board configuration

You only need to do this if you have a iphone between iPhone 6s - Iphone SE

  1. Install the App AX-CPU
  2. Open the app and look at the Model this is your board configuration save it for later.
Step 3: Saving blobs
  1. Open the TSSsaverwith safari
  2. Choose your device and check what ios versions are signed
  3. Go back to TSSsaver and fill out the information

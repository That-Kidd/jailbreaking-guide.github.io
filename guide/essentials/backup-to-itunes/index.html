Backing Up to iTunes

You must have a Mac or Windows PC to continue with the guide.
For this guide you will need the following;
  • iOS Device
  • Charge & Sync Cable for your iOS Device
  • Mac or Windows PC with enough storage for a backup
  • iTunes for Windows Users
Step 1: Backing up with iTunes
  1. On a Mac, open up iTunes
    2. iTunes is preinstalled on Macs and will either be in the dock or on the launchpad. Windows users can download iTunes here.
  2. Connect your iOS Device with its Charge & Sync cable to your Mac or PC.
    3. You may need a USB-C to Lightning Cable for newer Macs.
    If your device has been locked for more than an hour, you will be prompted to unlock your iOS device (iOS 12).
  3. Navigate to your iOS Device
  4. Check the box "Encrypt (iPad/iPhone/iPod Touch) Backup". You will be prompted to set a password.
    5. If you forget this password, you will not be able to restore your iOS Device with the backup.
  5. Click Back Up Now. This may take some time.
    6. If you do not have a sufficient amount of storage available on your computer, the backup will fail.
Once the backup finishes, you can continue to the Jailbreaking Guide.
